Earthquake Analysis Platform
REAL-TIME SEISMIC MONITORING & IMPACT ASSESSMENT
🎓 Educational & Research Tool

This platform is an open-source, browser-based tool for real-time earthquake monitoring, shaking intensity estimation, and seismicity analysis. It is designed for students, researchers, and educators in seismology, geophysics, and disaster risk management.

📡 Live USGS earthquake feeds 🌊 Shaking intensity zones ⏱ ak135 wave travel times 📊 Gutenberg-Richter analysis 🗺 Tectonic plate overlays 👥 Population exposure
⚠️
Important disclaimer
This tool uses point-source intensity prediction equations validated against USGS ShakeMap (moderate zone MAE ≈ 13%). Results are estimates for educational use only and must not be used for emergency response, structural engineering decisions, or official hazard assessment. Always refer to official USGS, national geological survey, and civil defence authority sources.
Data sources:  USGS FDSN Earthquake Catalog API  ·  WorldPop (Univ. Southampton)  ·  OpenStreetMap contributors  ·  Bird (2003) tectonic plates
Scientific methods: Atkinson & Wald (2007) IPE · Bilal & Askan (2014) IPE · Kennett et al. (1995) ak135 · Wells & Coppersmith (1994) · Aki (1965) G-R analysis
